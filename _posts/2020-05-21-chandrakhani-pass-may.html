Chandrakhani Pass the open pass to Malana / Partvati valley situated at an altitude of 3700 meters in kullu-manali in Himachal Pradesh .To get there you have two route's one is usual from Naggar to Rumsu to Naya tapru and then to Chandrakhani Pass . But there is another extra challenging route starts from Kotadhar Kais is the best route for chandrakhani pass. this trails is not recomended for begineers its a 3 day trek from Kotadhar if you are a good trekker .
